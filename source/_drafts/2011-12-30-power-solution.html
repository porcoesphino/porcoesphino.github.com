So, with my upcoming trip, and my relatively nomadic existence lately and my fondness for electronic toys, power is becoming an issue. In the short term, I'm buying a few phone batteries. These are almost required with smart phones these days as the battery can die during a night out. Especially if you get asked to throw up google maps or search the odd thing on the internet.

Long term though, I want a way to recharge my laptop. My phone can be done through my car or via usb.   The laptop isn't so easy. This is an apple product to power their macbooks during a plane flight. Apparently it won't charge them though. This is a pretty good blog entry on how to build up a solar charger from scratch for a Macbook. Mine is the air but I should be able to modify it. Here are some other points that might be useful. I'm not sure if I like the power supplies / solar panels they mention there though. The Sherpa 50 Adventure Kit seems like a better alternative. A few people also mention Voltaic Systems as a good place to source these types of goods.
